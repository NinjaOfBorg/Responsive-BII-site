Business Intelligence and Insights

We stand on the cusp of great things.

July 1, 2016 Andy Harris

Data Warehouses. Visualisations. Dashboarding. Business has always known that you don't know what yu don't measure. Billions of dollars have been spent on the collection and aggregation of data. Data Warehouses have sprung up in response to a desperate need for management to understand their business. The timely and accurate and meaningful vsualisation of all this data has always been lacking. With the advent of broad band internet, the use of the internet expanded to embrace rich multi media, connecting people with social media and generating a volume of data that was unthinkable. New technologies sprang up to cope with this information in real time. These technologies now threaten the very existence of companies like Oracle who have been forced to adapt their business model to compete with Open Source technologies that out perform traditional RDBMS systems at the large scale, "Big Data" end of the spectrum where all the big money used to be.

The paradigm shift.
DEWNR has been collecting data for decades and has had no way of visualising it in an affordable way. Until now. With new technologies like Hadoop, Cassandra, Neo4j and D3 we are nearing a point where we will be able to finally close the loop between the business and it's management. This will of course require new hardware and training but all at a fraction of the cost of traditional models.
